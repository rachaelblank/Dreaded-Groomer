Meet the Groomer


Nikki has been grooming dogs in the Phoenix area with over 7 years of experience. She has mastered the patience needed in handling difficult and nervous dogs in order to provide a true cage-free grooming experience. In addition to basic services, Nikki also offers advanced breed-specific cutting techniques and pet-safe fur dyeing experiences.


Nikki has three dogs of her own, Trixi, Tommy, and Doc. Trixi is a rescue and her breed is unknown. You can often see Trixi rocking different colors of the season. Tommy is a Great Pyramese Mountain Dog who requires regular grooming sessions to maintain the excessive amount of shedding and to keep his coat bright white. Doc is a Dachshund and the newest memeber to the family. Thankfully, he doesn't require much except for baths and nail trimming.
